Summary Supported PDFs Licenses and Help

You may import unmodified PDFs from The Arcane Library only. For personal use only.

For legal and technical reasons, this module only supports importing monster stats.

To see which PDFs are supported, select the "Supported PDFs" tab above.

Are you an author of third party Shadowdark content? I'd love to support your PDF! Open an issue on Github. Keep in mind that the PDF Importer can only support bestiaries (monsters) for now.
